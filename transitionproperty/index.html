Transition Property

Transition Property specialises in working with lettings agents, private landlords and property investors to providing good quality rooms to lets for working professional and international students in London

The company was formed around property professionals, with a vision of becoming a bespoke provider for short or long term lets with custom-tailored services to our clients

Landlords

  • Providing assured fixed monthly rent for up to 5 years, with no voids or loss of income. No additional management fees or commission to pay
  • No more unexpected calls from tenants.
  • We take over the legal and administrative work for you.
  • Maintaining condition of property
  • Dedicated team of experienced property professionals

Letting Agents

  • Assured fixed monthly rental income for up to 5 years.
  • Reduced time for arranging and attending viewings.
  • Your existing terms with landlords remain unaffected.
  • No late payments, eviction procedures and legal fees
  • A Dedicated Account Manager.

Tenants or International Student Lets

  • Professional service and care for each tenant.
  • A variety of very good standard properties available and shared with like-minded people
  • Bills included in the rental price
  • We cover prime Location in London. Our properties are within Zone 1 – 4
  • A consistent and friendly bespoke services tailored to individual requirements (international Students)
